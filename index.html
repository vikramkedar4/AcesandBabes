How It Works

Three steps to glory

01

Create Your Couple

Each partner signs up individually, then links accounts to form your team. Your couple gets a unified profile and starting ELO of 1200.

02

Log Your Matches

Play any game in our categories. Log the result — both couples must confirm before ELO updates. No disputed results, ever.

03

Climb the Ranks

Beat higher-ranked couples to gain more ELO. Defend your position. See where you stand globally, by city, and by game category.
